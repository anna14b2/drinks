Dark'N'Stormy

Ingredients

  1. 4 cl Goslings Black Seal Rum
  2. 1 cl Fresh Lime Juice
  3. 10 cl Goslings Ginger Beer
  4. 2 drops of Angostura

Instructions

  1. Into a tall glass filled with ice of Goslings Stormy Ginger Beer.
  2. Top with Goslings Black Seal Rum.
  3. 10 cl Goslings Ginger Beer
  4. Garnish with a lime wedge (optional).
